About Me

Born in Serbia, Veljko Pendic is a passionate front-end developer whose journey has been marked by a harmonious blend of technological innovation and a love for outdoor adventures. From the very outset, Veljko exhibited a curiosity for technology, drawn towards the enchanting world of coding and web development. Growing up in Serbia, Veljko's fascination with computers and programming languages emerged early in his life. Embracing the artistry of crafting seamless and visually appealing digital experiences, Veljko refined his skills, mastering the intricacies of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Beyond the realm of coding, Veljko finds solace and rejuvenation in the great outdoors. A fervent trail runner, he revels in the thrill of exploring nature's paths and challenging terrains. The adrenaline rush and the sense of accomplishment gained from conquering challenging trails fuel his spirit, providing a refreshing balance to his tech-oriented career. Moreover, Veljko's passion for sports extends to the soccer field. Whether it's a friendly game with friends or a competitive match, he finds joy and camaraderie in the beautiful game of soccer. The teamwork, strategy, and sheer exhilaration of the sport resonate deeply with his competitive spirit.
